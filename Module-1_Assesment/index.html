About Me

My name is JaBre' Jennings and I have a goal of becoming a Software Developer in 2020. I was born and raised in Baltimore, MD to JaVonica Powell and Keith Jennings. I am the oldest of 4 children. I attended college on a basketball scholarship to Newberry College in South Carolina but returned home to MD after one year. I enrolled in University of Maryland Eastern Shore to finish my collegiate education.

In 2013, I joined Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity Inc and graduated with my Bachelors in Sociology/Social Work. After graduation I returned to Baltimore to work at the Mayor's Office of Employment Development.

After 3 years at the Mayors Office, I decided to obtain my Masters in Software Engineering from UMUC. It was a huge leap of faith to pursue this degree with minimal prior knowledge but in the end I succeeded. On May 19, 2019 (My birthday) I walked across the graduation stage proud of myself with my family in attendance.
